ABOUT

Hi, I’m Ruthie. When I was a child, I loved art, stories and puzzles. Some things never change! Sure, I’m a few feet taller now. But I’ve grown into an open-minded problem-solver with a knack for storytelling and making things aesthetically pleasing.

Today, I am a UI/UX Designer. Through research, I’m able to empathize with users and identify their needs. I’m always striving to create human-centered experiences that are both beautiful and intuitive.
